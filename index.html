About Me

Hey, I’m Matt Haggard — a Christian, husband, dad, and guitar player. I believe in praising God with the strings (Psalm 150:4), and that’s what I’m here to do. My music blends faith, life experience, and rock and roll energy into something that I hope moves people — spiritually and emotionally.

Whether it's original songs about perseverance and truth, or covers of classics that shaped my sound, every note is played with purpose. Thanks for stopping by — I hope you hear something that sticks with you.
